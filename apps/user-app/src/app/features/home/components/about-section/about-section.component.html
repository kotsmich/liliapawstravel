Who We Are

Born from a love for rescue dogs

Lilia Paws Travel was founded in 2018 by Lilia Kovacs, a passionate animal welfare advocate who recognized a critical gap — thousands of dogs were being adopted across borders with no safe, reliable transport.

From a one-van operation to a network spanning 18 countries and 200+ shelters, we've safely transported over 1,200 dogs and counting.
