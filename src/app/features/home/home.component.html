Who We Are

Born from a love for rescue dogs

Lilia Paws Travel was founded in 2018 by Lilia Kovacs, a passionate animal welfare advocate who recognized a critical gap — thousands of dogs were being adopted across European borders with no safe, reliable way to get home.

What started as a one-van operation helping 10 dogs per month has grown into a coordinated network spanning 18 countries, working with over 200 shelters and rescue organizations. We've safely transported over 1,200 dogs and counting.

Every single trip is personal to us. We know each dog's name, their story, and the family waiting for them on the other side.
